Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "Database Connection"?

A database connection is a measure of the number of devices that are using your app's database simultaneously. This isn't the same as the total number of users of your app or visitors to your site. It's any open network connection to our database servers, including streaming REST API requests. All our plans have a hard limit on the number of database connections. Our Free and Prototype plans are limited to 100 .So did with launch plan to 500. The limit cannot be raised. The Unlimited plans have a courtesy limit of 10,000 database connections. This can be removed to permanently allow Unlimited connections if you email us at hello@cloudboost.io . The reason we impose this courtesy limit is to prevent abuse and to ensure that we are prepared to handle our largest customers. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance so we can lift this limit and ensure we have enough capacity available for your needs.

Can I see my detailed metrics?

Yes. Log in to your dashboard and you can.

Do you provide a backup of my data?

We automatically backup your data twice a day and replicate your data every single time you save to three availability zones in 3 different data-centers across US, Europe and Asia. We also let you access your MongoDB database natively so you can export your data anytime you like.

Where can I get CloudBoost Developers?

Any developer can build apps on CloudBoost. We have SDK's launched on multiple languages. You can check them out here. We also have a consulting team who consults and builds your project for you. Feel free to talk to us if you want to choose that route.

Do you have an SLA?

Yes. We do. You can check it out here.

What if I exceed my paid plan limits?

We have a scale plan to suit you. If you're looking for an enterprise offering. Let us know.

What kind of support will I receive?

All paid CloudBoost Accounts come with email and phone support from CloudBoost engineers. Additional levels of support, including 24x7 phone support, are available to Enterprise customers.
